# GramVartha Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-20

GramVartha helps citizens access village notices, government scheme guidance, weather advisory information, and complaint tracking.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use the app:

- Name and phone number when you create or use a citizen account.
- Village selection or scanned village QR information saved on your device and sent to the server when needed for village-specific services.
- Complaint details that you submit, including text, category, status, optional photo evidence, timestamp, and location.
- Camera access when you scan a village QR code or capture complaint evidence.
- Location access when you choose to attach your location to a complaint.
- Selected photos when you attach complaint evidence.
- Push notification token to send village notices, complaint updates, and service updates.
- Basic app interaction and technical data needed to operate, secure, and improve the service.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- Provide village notices and local services.
- Register and authenticate citizen accounts.
- Submit, manage, and track complaints.
- Attach evidence and location to complaints when you choose to provide them.
- Send notifications about notices, complaints, and important updates.
- Improve reliability, safety, and support.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with:

- Authorized village or administrative staff who handle notices, complaints, and local services.
- Service providers that operate the app backend, hosting, storage, or notifications.
- Authorities when required by law.

## Data Security

We use HTTPS for network communication in production and take reasonable measures to protect user data. Authentication tokens are stored using secure device storage where supported.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Complaint, account, and notice-related data may be retained while needed to provide the service, comply with legal requirements, or maintain administrative records.
